Skedule is a line-bot application to help you with the main functionality of its weather forecast and reminder. Everyday, Skedule will give you the weather forecast and remind you to bring the umbrella or just enjoy the sunny day. By using Skedule, you could add your schedule and custom your reminder setting. The schedule consists of title, description, date, time, and priority. You could invite Skedule to your group, add your group's schedule by anyone of the group, and Skedule will remind you and your friends in time. You could use Skedule for your personal schedule, exam schedule, hang-out time, and so forth. Skedule will remind you with our real time server. You also could set the priority of your schedule, show your schedule with the filter, and edit them anytime. Try Skedule now! Skedule, never miss anything anymore.
